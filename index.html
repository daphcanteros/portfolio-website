About Me

I am Daphane Canteros. You can call me Daph. Building simple websites with WordPress and Wix sparked my interest in Front-End Web Development.

The desire to learn how to build UI based on a desired output continuously drove me to learn as much as I could. This is when I began studying JavaScript and HTML/CSS.

After learning the fundamentals, I applied React, Tailwind, Bootstrap, and Node.js. I developed these passion projects during my self-learning journey.

My goal is to be part of a competitive and collaborative team that builds and tests a functional system that adds value to clients.
